A Few Accomplishments

The time I spent in the academy and actively participating in Organizations provided us with challenges and projects that sparked to our creative minds.

IoT Based Rice Crop Monitor Webpage (Realtime Monitoring)

This was a product of our Thesis to use Electronics Engineering to assist the Agricultural field, monitoring the parameters that can affect the quality of our staple food - Rice, by means of sensors deployed on the field. This data can be accessed by farmers(to alarm them for the ideal time for pest control, watering, etc.), Agriculturists and other researchers as well.

E-YOUTH ICONS: SUMOBOT

We managed to grab the Championship throne in a sumobot competition that was held on school grounds which later on escalated to participation in a Sumobot competion during the National IECEP Anniversary at University of the Philippines. We failed to claim the victory, but we were happy that our small project put up a fight.

Making of Iot Rice Crop Monitor

This is the output device of our thesis. It involves various sensors, a monitor, a printed circuit board, and a microcontroller filled with codes to function as it was intended to. The aim was to minimize the cost for farmers - our main beneficiary, to afford the device. It will minimize the use of resources they put on pesticides, travel time, watering, etc.
